Sustainability Practices

Composting

We have implemented a composting program to reduce food waste and promote sustainability. All organic waste from our kitchen is collected and composted to produce nutrient-rich soil for local farms and gardens.

Waste Reduction

We are committed to reducing waste throughout our operations. We encourage customers to bring their reusable containers for takeout orders, and we use eco-friendly packaging made from recyclable and biodegradable materials.

Energy-Efficient Transportation

We prioritize energy-efficient transportation methods for deliveries. Our delivery fleet consists of electric vehicles and bicycles to minimize carbon emissions and promote clean transportation.

Partnerships with Environmental Organizations

We actively partner with local environmental organizations to support and participate in initiatives aimed at preserving the environment. Together, we organize community clean-up events and promote environmental education programs.
